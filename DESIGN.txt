ADVERSARY - Master Design & Development Plan

Vision Statement
Adversary is a single-screen platformer in the tradition of Donkey Kong, filtered through the
death mechanics of Dark Souls and the gothic atmosphere of early Castlevania, with subtle
references to Dark Souls lore woven into its four levels. It targets the aesthetic, feel, and sound
of a lost 1984-1986 NES cartridge. Built in Phaser.js for browser play, architected from day one
for Electron packaging and eventual Steam distribution.

Tech Stack
  Game Engine:       Phaser 3 - Purpose-built for 2D, handles tilemaps, physics, audio, input
  Language:          JavaScript (ES6+) - Portable, shareable, good Phaser ecosystem
  Desktop Wrapper:   Electron - Wraps to .exe/.app for Steam
  Steam Integration: Greenworks (Steamworks SDK) - Achievements, leaderboards, cloud saves
  Save System:       Abstracted SaveManager - Swaps between localStorage and Steam Cloud
  Asset Pipeline:    Texture atlases / sprite sheets - NES-style, loaded via Phaser asset loader
  Audio:             Phaser + Web Audio API - Chiptune-ready, can use .ogg/.mp3 tracker exports

Game States / Scene List
  1. BootScene - Preload minimal assets, set up display scaling
  2. PreloadScene - Load all assets with a simple loading bar
  3. TitleScene - Title screen with flashing "PRESS ANY KEY TO START" prompt
  4. GameScene - Core gameplay (instantiated per level via LevelRegistry)
  5. PauseScene - Overlay pause (inventory/equipment management planned for Phase 7)
  6. InterludeScene - Brief interlude between levels (stub, Phase 7 expansion)
  7. LoopCompleteScene - Loop completion screen with loop number
  8. GameOverScene - Game over, displays final score, transitions to high scores
  9. HighScoreScene - High score table (top 10, initials entry planned for Phase 7)

Level Themes & Dark Souls References
  Level 1: "The Ashen Hearth"   - Ruined bonfire shrine, warm amber tones. (Firelink Shrine)
           Checkpoint: Lit bonfire at top
  Level 2: "Cresthollow"        - Winding urban ruins, fog, archers on ledges. (Undead Burg)
           Checkpoint: Tower gate
  Level 3: "The Iron Passage"   - Gears, traps, pendulum blades, iron architecture. (Sen's Fortress)
           Checkpoint: Iron fog gate
  Level 4: "The Pale Spire"     - Cathedral, grand hall, boss arena. (Anor Londo)
           Checkpoint: Defeat the Hollow King

Player Attributes & Systems
  Core Stats:
    HP - starts at 1 max, increases via level up
    XP - accumulates from kills, held in soul on death (no XP recovery = no heal)
    Mana - starts at 6, increases via level up or drops
    Level - gates attribute point allocation on level up

  Level Up Choices (pick one per level):
    Increase max HP by 1
    Increase attack power by 1
    Increase max mana by 3
    (Speed boost option planned but not yet implemented)

  Gear Slots:
    Weapon (affects attack damage) - Rusty Sword (+1), Iron Blade (+2), Knight Saber (+3)
    Armor (chance to negate 1 damage per hit) - Leather Vest (+1), Chain Mail (+2), Plate Armor (+3)
    Accessory (special passive effect) - not yet implemented

Controls (Castlevania 1 inspired)
  Keyboard:
    Arrow Keys     Move / Climb ladders
    Down           Duck
    Z              Jump
    X              Attack (sword swing)
    X while duck   Low attack
    X while air    Air attack
    Down+X (air)   Plunging attack (2x damage, bounces on hit)
    Up+X           Special attack (costs mana)
    C              Dodge roll (brief i-frames)
    Enter          Pause

  Gamepad:
    D-Pad / Left Stick   Move / Climb
    A Button (0)         Jump
    X Button (2)         Attack
    Start (9)            Pause

  Debug (will be removed):
    G                    Trigger Game Over
    L                    Trigger Loop Complete
    Gamepad L1           Skip Level

Hit Reaction / Knockback
  When the player takes damage, they are knocked backward and upward in the air - identical in
  feel to Castlevania 1. The knockback can send the player off platforms, into hazards, or into
  enemies, making positioning and terrain awareness critical. Knockback direction is always away
  from the damage source. The player has 1.5s invincibility frames after being hit (i-frames) to
  prevent chain damage. Enemies have their own lighter knockback reactions when struck.
  Ring of Fog accessory extends i-frames by 600ms (not yet implemented).

Death & Soul Mechanic
  1. Player dies - soul orb spawns at last safe ground position (clamped to nearest valid
     platform surface if needed)
  2. Player respawns at stage start with 1 HP, gear retained
  3. Player reaches soul - XP restored (NO health recovery - intentional design decision)
  4. Player dies again before reaching soul - GAME OVER
  5. Soul is visible on screen as a pulsing orb
  6. HUD shows directional arrow pointing toward off-screen soul

The Phantom - Anti-Grinding Mechanic
  Invincible ghost that appears if the player lingers too long without climbing higher. Passes
  through all geometry, deals 2 damage on contact (1.5s cooldown). Retreats when player
  climbs 40px above trigger position. Telegraphed by 2.5s "!! DANGER !!" text warning.
  Base trigger: 20s idle timer. Scales with loop (-3s per loop, min 8s).
  Disabled in Level 4 (boss level).

Special Attacks (Castlevania pickup-swap system)
  Knife        1 mana   Fast horizontal projectile, low damage
  Axe          2 mana   Arc throw upward then falls with gravity, spins visually
  Holy Water   2 mana   Short lob, creates damaging ground fire on impact
  Cross        2 mana   Boomerang - flies out and returns to player, hits twice
  Skull Key    3 mana   Slow spiralling projectile, pierces enemies
  Ember Flask  4 mana   Arcs forward, explodes on platform impact for area damage

  Note: Original design had Holy Water at 3 mana and Cross at 3 mana. Adjusted during
  implementation for balance. Pickup cycling system not yet implemented.

Items & Drops
  Drop Frequency (from enemies):
    Mana Shards (most common) - restore 1-2 mana
    Hearts (uncommon) - restore 1 HP
    Weapon drops (rare) - improve attack tier
    Armor drops (rare) - improve damage negation
    Special attack pickups (rare) - cycle current special weapon

  Items pop upward on drop, fall with gravity, bounce, collide with platforms.
  8s lifetime, blink during last 2s. Yellow flash on player confirms pickup.

  Accessories (NOT YET IMPLEMENTED):
    Rusted Ring - slight speed boost
    Hawk Ring - increases special attack range (projectile lifetime extension IS implemented)
    Chloranthy Ring - faster mana regen
    Covetous Ring - increased drop rates
    Hornet Ring - critical hit chance on plunging attack
    Ring of Fog - extended invincibility frames when taking damage

  Breakable Objects: Crates and barrels placed at fixed positions per level. One hit to break.
  25 score bonus. Drop table: 35% heart, 50% mana shard, 8% special attack pickup.

Chests & Mimics (Post-Loop 1)
  Only active when currentLoop >= 2. 15% base chance to spawn a chest when an enemy dies,
  +10% bonus if 2+ kills within 2 seconds.

  Normal Chests: 12x10 brown/gold rectangles. 15s lifetime, blink last 3s. 50 score bonus on
  open. Generous drops: 30% weapon, 30% armor, 50% heart, 100% mana shard fallback.

  Mimics: 25% of spawned chests on loop 2, +8% per additional loop, capped at 65%.
  Tell: subtle breathing scale pulse (1.03x/0.97x). Ambush deals 3 damage if touched without
  attacking first. 4 HP, fast chase AI (70px/s). Guaranteed rare+ drops on kill.

Bestiary

  IMPLEMENTED ENEMIES:

  Hollow Soldier - 1 HP, 1 dmg, 10 XP, 50 score
    Basic melee patrol. 30px/s walk, 50px/s chase within 60px. Platform edge detection.
    400ms attack windup, 900ms cooldown. Drops: 45% mana, 15% heart.

  Hollow Archer - 1 HP, 1 dmg, 15 XP, 75 score
    Stationary ranged. Fires horizontal arrows (100px/s) when player within 120px H + 30px V.
    Some archers can shoot diagonally downward. 2.5s cooldown. Arrows can be ducked under.
    Drops: 50% mana, 6% special.

  Hollow Knight - 3 HP, 2 dmg, 30 XP, 150 score
    Tougher melee with ladder climbing (40% chance). 22px/s patrol, 38px/s chase.
    550ms attack windup, 1200ms cooldown. Drops: 25% heart, 5% weapon, 5% armor.

  Skeleton - 1 HP, 1 dmg, 20 XP, 80 score
    Reassembles once after death (1.5s delay, awards only 25% score on first kill).
    Second death or special attack kill is permanent. Same AI as Hollow Soldier.
    Drops (permanent death only): 45% mana.

  Gargoyle - 2 HP, 1 dmg, 20 XP, 100 score
    Flying swooper. Patrols horizontally at set altitude (40px/s). Swoops diagonally at 90px/s
    when player passes below. Low/duck attacks cannot reach it. Drops: 40% mana.

  Bat (Flying Hazard) - killable, 30 score, 5 XP
    Flies horizontally across screen near player height. Bobs in sine wave pattern.
    Speed, amplitude, and spawn rate scale with loop. Not a full enemy - managed by
    FlyingHazardSystem.

  Mimic - 4 HP, 2 dmg (3 on ambush), 40 XP, 200 score
    See Chests & Mimics section. Fast aggressive chase (70px/s) after transformation.

  BOSS - The Hollow King (Level 4):
    20 HP (loop 1), +8 HP per loop. 16x26 deep purple rectangle.
    Loop scaling: +1 damage, +10% speed per loop.

    Attack patterns:
      Slash - Close-range melee. 400ms windup, 650ms recovery window.
      Leap - Mid-range jump attack. 500ms windup, 3 damage, 900ms recovery.
      Charge - Dash across arena. 600ms windup with telegraph, 180px/s.
               Jumpable (10px vertical hit check). Single-hit per dash.
               1000ms recovery (1500ms if hits wall).
      Shield Bash (loop 2+) - AoE close-range. 350ms windup, 700ms recovery. 35% chance.

    No passive contact damage - only hurts during active attack states.
    Cannot be staggered by normal attacks (no HURT state).
    4 weak points in arena - hitting all 4 triggers 3s stagger with 2x damage multiplier.
    Death sequence: multi-flash, camera shakes, 2x scale fade.
    Drops: 100% heart, 40% weapon, 40% armor.

  NOT YET IMPLEMENTED:
    Silver Knight - faster Armored Knight variant
    Painting Guardian - fast and aggressive
    Titanite Demon - rare, powerful
    Darkmoon Soldier - ranged with special projectiles

Traps & Environmental Hazards

  Rolling Skulls (HazardSystem) - DK-style rolling hazards
    Spawned from stone head structures. Roll across platforms, fall off edges, reverse direction
    on landing. Can descend ladders using DK-style probability algorithm (factors in player
    position, input direction, difficulty). Jump over for 50 score bonus.
    Loop 2+: "Wild" skulls (reddish) bounce diagonally and loosely track player.
    Speed, spawn rate, and ladder aggression scale with loop.

  Pendulum Blades (TrapSystem) - Swinging ceiling-mounted blades
    Sine wave swing motion. 1 damage, 1s contact cooldown.
    Time your movement to pass at the peak of the swing.

  Dart Traps (TrapSystem) - Wall-mounted arrow launchers
    Fire darts at regular intervals. 1 damage, consumed on hit.
    Jump over for 25 score bonus. 3s lifetime.

  Flying Bats (FlyingHazardSystem) - See Bat entry in Bestiary.

Scoring System
  Kills grant base XP and score (enemy-dependent)
  Jump-over bonuses: skulls (+50), darts (+25), arrows (+50)
  Breakable objects: +25
  Chest open: +50
  Level completion time bonus (not yet implemented)
  Soul retrieval score multiplier (not yet implemented)
  Top 10 high scores saved. 3-character initials entry planned (Phase 7).

Loop System
  Completing all 4 levels triggers Loop Complete screen, then restarts from Level 1.
  Player retains: gear, level, attributes, XP, current special attack.
  Each loop: enemy HP/damage scale up, spawn rates increase, wild skulls appear,
  Phantom trigger timer shortens, bat speed/amplitude increase.
  Mimic chance increases per loop. Shield Bash unlocked on loop 2+.
  Loop count displayed on HUD when loop > 1.

  NOT YET IMPLEMENTED: platform randomization, loop-exclusive enemies, score multipliers,
  combo counters, environmental easter eggs, attract-mode demo.

Save System Architecture (SaveManager)
  SaveManager.saveHighScores(scores) / .loadHighScores()
  SaveManager.saveSettings(settings) / .loadSettings()
  Browser: localStorage. Electron: Steam Cloud via greenworks or local file fallback.
  Game code never calls localStorage directly.

HUD
  HP hearts (colored squares, red=full, dark red=empty), max 20.
  MP, XP, LV, Special attack labels.
  Score (top-right), Loop indicator (top-center, shown if loop > 1).
  Soul directional arrow when soul is off-screen.
  Boss health bar (bottom of screen, Level 4 only) with color shift at HP thresholds.

Level-Up System
  XP thresholds: 80, 200, 400, 700, 1100, 1600, 2200, 2900, 3700 (then +900/level).
  Two-phase UI: "LEVEL UP" announcement splash, then choice menu with confirm step.
  Game freezes during selection (physics paused). 800ms freeze after confirming choice
  before physics resumes. 1.5s i-frames granted on resume to prevent cheap deaths.

In-Browser Instruction Manual
  NES-style instruction booklet built into the game page. Toggled via "MANUAL" tab on the
  left side of the game screen. Covers story, controls, gameplay mechanics, special attacks,
  full bestiary, traps, items, equipment, all four worlds, and debug controls.
  Marked as work in progress.

Asset Integration Plan (Phase 8 - NOT YET STARTED)
  All assets loaded via Phaser's asset loader from an /assets directory. Sprite sheets use
  texture atlases (JSON + PNG). Currently using colored rectangle placeholders for all sprites.

  Sprite categories needed:
    Player: idle, walk, jump, duck, attack, air attack, plunge, hurt/knockback, death, soul orb
    Per enemy: idle, walk, attack, death
    Chest: closed, opening, Mimic idle, Mimic reveal, Mimic walk, Mimic attack
    Phantom: idle, move (semi-transparent, ghostly)
    Environment: tileset per level (floor, wall, platform, ladder, background, breakables)
    UI: HUD elements, NES-style bitmap font, menu screens, heart/mana icons
    Effects: projectiles, impacts, soul orb glow, level up flash, Phantom warning overlay

  NES Palette Constraint: Limit to the 54 NES hardware colors. Max 4 colors per background
  tile, max 3 colors + transparency per sprite.

Audio Plan (Phase 8 - NOT YET STARTED)

    Title theme, Level 1-3 theme (shared or per-level), Level 4/boss theme,
